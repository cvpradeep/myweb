The Grand Marginal Seas Adventure

Join the Straw Hat Pirates as they journey across the world's marginal seas, facing challenges and earning treasure clues!

Adventure Story

Pacific Ocean Adventure

The adventure begins in the vast **Pacific Ocean**, where the Straw Hat Pirates sail across the **Bering Sea**. Here, they encounter icy waters and fierce winds as they fight off sea monsters. The crew then navigates to the **Gulf of California**, facing treacherous whirlpools and navigating between narrow cliffs.

As they continue, they enter the warm waters of the **Philippine Sea**, where Nami’s navigation skills are tested against powerful storms. The journey takes them through the **South China Sea**, where they must escape pirate hunters. Finally, the crew sails to the **Tasman Sea**, battling fierce ocean currents and sea creatures to earn their first treasure clue.

Ocean Marginal Seas
Pacific Ocean Bering Sea, Gulf of California, Philippine Sea, South China Sea, Tasman Sea

Atlantic Ocean Adventure

The crew moves on to the **Atlantic Ocean**, where they sail into the **Caribbean Sea**. Here, they face rival pirates and must fight for control of a treasure map. Their next challenge takes place in the **North Sea**, where Zoro duels with a sword master aboard a floating fortress.

As they continue, the crew navigates through the powerful storms of the **Gulf of Mexico**. Sanji must cook a special dish to appease the sea guardian in the **Mediterranean Sea**. Finally, they sail through the icy waters of the **Labrador Sea**, where Brook plays a song to calm the dangerous waters and earn their second treasure clue.

Ocean Marginal Seas
Atlantic Ocean Caribbean Sea, North Sea, Gulf of Mexico, Mediterranean Sea, Labrador Sea

Indian Ocean Adventure

In the **Indian Ocean**, the crew sails into the **Arabian Sea**, where they face a powerful sea god who controls the tides. Their journey continues through the **Red Sea**, where Usopp must shoot down floating targets to guide the ship through a dangerous strait.

As they sail into the **Bay of Bengal**, Luffy battles a giant sea monster to protect the ship. The crew also crosses the **Persian Gulf** and the **Gulf of Oman**, where Nami uses her Clima-Tact to control the wild weather, leading the crew to their third treasure clue.

Ocean Marginal Seas
Indian Ocean Arabian Sea, Red Sea, Bay of Bengal, Persian Gulf, Gulf of Oman

Arctic Ocean Adventure

Next, the crew ventures into the frigid **Arctic Ocean**, where they must sail through the **Barents Sea**. Here, Robin deciphers ancient runes hidden in the ice to unlock the next path. The journey continues through the **Kara Sea**, where the crew battles a snowstorm and must fend off ice giants.

In the **Laptev Sea**, Brook uses his soul powers to protect the crew from ghostly sea creatures. They also travel through the **East Siberian Sea** and the **Beaufort Sea**, where Luffy must use his Gomu Gomu abilities to break free from icy traps and secure the fourth treasure clue.

Ocean Marginal Seas
Arctic Ocean Barents Sea, Kara Sea, Laptev Sea, East Siberian Sea, Beaufort Sea

Southern Ocean Adventure

Finally, the crew sails into the cold waters of the **Southern Ocean**. They navigate through the icy **Ross Sea**, where Zoro must slice through glaciers blocking the ship's path. The crew enters the **Weddell Sea**, where they face a fierce snowstorm that threatens to freeze the ship.

In the **Amundsen Sea**, Sanji cooks a warm meal to keep the crew’s spirits high as they battle harsh conditions. With teamwork and determination, the crew secures the final treasure clue, completing their journey across the world's marginal seas.

Ocean Marginal Seas
Southern Ocean Ross Sea, Weddell Sea, Amundsen Sea
