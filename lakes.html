The Grand Lakes Adventure

Join the Straw Hat Pirates as they journey across the world's greatest lakes, facing challenges and earning treasure clues!

Adventure Story

Zoro's Training Begins - Asia

In Asia, Zoro faces a tough test at Lake Baikal, where he must cut through the thick fog using his cursed sword. With his mentor watching from a distance, Zoro must master his skill to earn the first treasure clue!

Continent Lakes
Asia Baikal, Balkhash, Lop Nor, Aral Sea, Chilika

Nami's Weather-Control Test - Europe

Nami must control wild weather at Lake Lucerne. Using her skills to calm the storm, she helps the crew rescue Robin, who has been captured by the lake's guardian. Can they save her in time?

Continent Lakes
Europe Onega, Ladoga, Constance, Vanern, Lucerne

Luffy's Battle with Queen Victoria - Africa

Luffy faces Queen Victoria at Lake Victoria. In a fierce battle, with whirlpools and crashing waves, Luffy must use his full strength to defeat her and gain the third treasure clue!

Continent Lakes
Africa Chad, Victoria, Tanganyika, Malawi

Sanji's High-Speed Duel - North America

In North America, Sanji must face Doflamingo at Lake Erie. Using his Sky Walk, Sanji dodges Doflamingo's attacks in mid-air. Can Sanji secure the fourth clue?

Continent Lakes
North America Superior, Michigan, Huron, Erie, Ontario

Zoro's Strength Test - South America

Zoro faces his next challenge at Lake Titicaca in South America, where he must slice through huge rock formations with his cursed sword to earn the next treasure clue.

Continent Lakes
South America Titicaca, Poopo

Final Test - Australia

In Australia, Zoro faces his final test at Lake Eyre. In the middle of a violent windstorm, Zoro must cut through the tornado-like winds to prove his mastery of the sword and earn the final treasure clue!

Continent Lakes
Australia Eyre, Torrens, Moore
