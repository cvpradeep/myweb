The Grand Rivers Adventure

Join the Straw Hat Pirates as they journey across the world's greatest rivers, facing challenges and earning treasure clues!

Adventure Story

Luffy's Battle at the Ganga - Asia

The Straw Hat Pirates start their adventure in **Asia**, where they encounter the mighty **Ganga River**. Luffy faces a river guardian who controls the powerful currents and whirlpools of the Ganga. As the crew continues their journey, they sail past the **Indus**, **Chang Jiang**, **Huang He**, and **Ob** rivers, each presenting its own challenges.

Luffy must use his Gomu Gomu powers to battle the whirlpools and defeat the river guardian to get the first treasure clue. The crew’s journey through these powerful rivers brings them face to face with the strength of nature itself.

Continent Rivers
Asia Ganga, Indus, Chang Jiang, Huang He, Ob

Nami's Navigation Challenge at the Danube - Europe

In **Europe**, the crew sails along the **Danube River**, but a fierce storm stirs up the waters. **Nami** must use her navigation skills to guide the ship through the dangerous waters of the Danube. As they pass the **Rhine**, **Rhone**, **Dnieper**, and **Volga** rivers, they face wild currents and thunderstorms created by the guardians of each river.

Using her Clima-Tact, Nami controls the weather, calming the storm and helping the crew safely navigate through the rivers. They earn the second treasure clue after navigating these famous European rivers.

Continent Rivers
Europe Danube, Rhine, Rhone, Dnieper, Volga

Zoro's Sword Test at the Nile - Africa

Arriving in **Africa**, Zoro faces a daunting task at the **Nile River**. He must slice through powerful water torrents created by the Nile’s guardian. As they journey along the **Congo**, **Niger**, **Zambezi**, and **Orange** rivers, the crew encounters whirlpools, rapids, and raging waters.

Zoro uses his three-sword style to cut through the torrents and obstacles, earning the third treasure clue while showcasing his incredible strength and skill.

Continent Rivers
Africa Nile, Congo, Niger, Zambezi, Orange

Usopp's Strategic Challenge at the Mississippi - North America

The crew heads to **North America**, where they must navigate the winding waters of the **Mississippi River**. Along the way, they also face challenges from the **Missouri**, **St. Lawrence**, **Mackenzie**, and **Colorado** rivers, where Usopp’s sharpshooting skills are put to the test.

Usopp must shoot down floating targets and clear dangerous logs from the river while avoiding rapids and earning the fourth treasure clue.

Continent Rivers
North America Mississippi, Missouri, St Lawrence, Mackenzie, Colorado

Sanji's Cooking Duel at the Amazon - South America

In **South America**, Sanji faces a unique challenge along the **Amazon River**, where the river guardian challenges him to a cooking duel. As the crew passes the **Orinoco**, **Parana**, and **Paraguay** rivers, Sanji gathers rare ingredients from the jungle to prepare a dish that will impress the guardian.

His cooking skills are put to the test, and by winning the duel, Sanji secures the fifth treasure clue.

Continent Rivers
South America Amazon, Orinoco, Parana, Paraguay

Brook's Musical Test at the Murray - Australia

For the final challenge, the crew arrives in **Australia**, where **Brook** must play his violin to calm the guardian of the **Murray River**. As they journey along the **Darling River**, Brook’s music soothes mystical creatures and calms the waters.

Through his enchanting song, Brook wins the final treasure clue and completes the journey across the world’s rivers.

Continent Rivers
Australia Murray, Darling
